Projects

Samosa Selector

Samosa Selector is a simple clicker game built using React.js, where players click a samosa image to earn samosas. Players can use their samosas to purchase upgrades that increase the number of samosas earned per click.

  • Double Stuffed: Doubles the click multiplier for 10 samosas.
  • Party Pack: Increases the click multiplier 5x for 100 samosas.
  • Full Feast: Increases the click multiplier 10x for 1000 samosas.

Game of Thrones Quiz

This interactive Game of Thrones Quiz tests your knowledge of the legendary series with 10 challenging questions. Flip the flashcards to reveal the answers, and navigate between questions using the Previous and Next buttons. Themed with a medieval fantasy design, moving backgrounds, and the official GOT title, this quiz immerses you in the world of Westeros!
